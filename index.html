FAQ's

A Fractional CTO is a senior technology leader who works with your business on a part-time or project basis. They bring the same strategic expertise and leadership as a full-time CTO, but at a fraction of the cost and commitment.

If you're experiencing rapid growth, struggling with technical debt, planning a major technology upgrade, or simply need senior technical guidance without the overhead of a full-time hire, it's likely the right time to bring in a FCTO.

Costs vary depending on the scope and duration of engagement. A FCTO typically costs a fraction of a full-time CTO salary, with flexible arrangements available from a few hours per week to several days. Get in touch for a tailored quote.

Engagements are entirely flexible. Some businesses need support for a specific project lasting a few weeks, while others benefit from ongoing strategic guidance over months or years. There are no lengthy notice periods or long-term commitments required.

A FCTO works alongside your existing team, providing mentorship, technical leadership, and strategic direction. They adapt to your workflows, tools, and culture to ensure a seamless fit without disrupting your current operations.

A consultant typically delivers recommendations and reports. A FCTO rolls up their sleeves and works hands-on with your team. They take ownership of technical strategy, lead implementation, mentor developers, and are accountable for outcomes, not just advice.
